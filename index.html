Welcome to the BCC vel victores vel victi, bibamus tamen!
Playing cricket in Berlin since 1985! If you would like to watch or play cricket then please get in contact with us using our contact form .
Matches

Games take place most summer weekends and start at 11am at the Maifeld in Berlin-Charlottenburg and normally last until 6pm. The nearest train station is S-Bahn Berlin-Pichelsberg.

Training

If you would like to play for us or just come along to learn cricket then we train every Thursday from 5pm at the above address. All you need to bring is a pair of training shoes (no spikes) and sports clothing.

Facilities

Shower and toilet facilities are available. Special protective cricket equipment can be supplied by the BCC. Our ground is also ideal for young children - no traffic and no dogs!
